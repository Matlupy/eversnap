Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

EverSnap ("the app") is a status saver for WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

EverSnap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No account is required to use the app. We do not have access to your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or any information about the statuses you view or save.

2. File Access

The app requests read access to your device storage solely to display and save WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business statuses. Files you choose to save are stored locally on your device in the EverSnap folder. No files are uploaded to any server.

3. Advertising

Free tier users see banner and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings under Google → Ads. Premium subscribers see no ads.

4. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing and managed by RevenueCat. Payment information is handled entirely by Google and is never seen or stored by EverSnap.

5. Background Service

Premium users may enable auto-save, which runs a foreground service to monitor and save new statuses before they expire. This service only accesses the WhatsApp status folders on your device and does not transmit any data.

6. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play Services — in-app billing

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their data use.

7. Children's Privacy

EverSnap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
