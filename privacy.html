Privacy Policy

Company: EverSnap
Applies to: All apps and products by EverSnap
Effective date: 9 April 2026
Last updated: 9 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EverSnap handles your information across all of our apps and products. We take privacy seriously — our apps are designed to work entirely offline where possible, and process all files locally on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

EverSnap apps do not collect, transmit, store, or share:

  • The contents of any file you process (PDFs, images, documents, etc.)
  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Device identifiers, location data, or contacts
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

All file processing happens on your device. Your documents never leave your phone.

2. Purchases and Billing

Some EverSnap apps offer in-app subscriptions or purchases. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment is processed entirely by Google Play Billing or Apple App Store. We never see your payment details.
  • We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription entitlement using an anonymised purchase token. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • No personal data beyond the anonymous purchase token is sent to any server.

3. Crash Reporting

EverSnap apps use Firebase Crashlytics (Google) to collect anonymised crash reports. These reports contain device model, OS version, and a stack trace — they do not contain any file contents or personal information. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions

Each app requests only the permissions it needs to function. Common permissions and their purposes:

PermissionWhy it is needed
READ / WRITE storageOpen and save files you choose
CAMERA (optional)Capture photos or scan documents
INTERNETVerify subscription status via RevenueCat; send crash reports via Crashlytics

5. Children's Privacy

EverSnap apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
