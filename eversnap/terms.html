1. About EverSnap

EverSnap is a WhatsApp status saver application that allows you to view, save, and optionally share WhatsApp statuses posted by your contacts before they expire. The app reads status media that is already stored on your device by WhatsApp and saves copies to your chosen folder.

EverSnap does not intercept network traffic, bypass encryption, or access WhatsApp servers. It only reads files from the local storage location that WhatsApp itself writes to on your Android device.
